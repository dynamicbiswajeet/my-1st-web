In a small coastal town, where the cliffs kissed the roaring sea, a young girl 
    named Mira discovered an ancient, weathered compass buried in the sand. Intrigued, she took it home, unaware that it held the 
    power to reveal hidden truths about her family's past. As Mira followed the compass's needle, she was led on a series of adventures 
    that unraveled long-forgotten stories of love, loss, and courage. Each destination unveiled a piece of her heritage, connecting her to 
    the spirits of her ancestors. With every step, Mira learned not only about her roots but also about her own strength, ultimately leading her to a choice that 
    would shape her future and her town's legacy.
